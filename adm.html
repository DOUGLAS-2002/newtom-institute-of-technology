Admissions

Admissions Process

Follow these simple steps to join Greenwood College

1

Complete Application

Fill out our online application form with your personal and academic information.

2

Submit Documents

Upload required documents including academic transcripts and identification.

3

Application Review

Our admissions team will review your application within 5-7 business days.

4

Admission Decision

You'll receive an email notification with your admission status.

5

Enrollment & Payment

Complete enrollment by paying tuition fees or setting up a payment plan.

6

Orientation

Attend orientation to get acquainted with campus life and resources.
