Shaping Futures Through Quality Education

Newton Institute Of Technology is a premier modern technical college accredited by TVETA and NTSA specializing in ICT, Plant Operator, Driving, Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy, and Music. We are located in Migori town along Migori-Sirare Highway, 0.3km opposite Rubis Filling Station.
