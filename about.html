Our History

Founded in 2010, Newton Institute of Technology began as a small technical college in Migori with just 2 programs: ICT and Hairdressing. Through strategic partnerships with industry leaders and TVETA accreditation in 2015, we've grown to offer 5 accredited programs serving over 1,200 students annually.

Key Milestones:

  • 2010: Institute founded with initial 2 programs
  • 2015: Received full TVETA accreditation
  • 2018: Expanded campus facilities by 200%
  • 2021: Launched industry partnership program
  • 2023: Recognized as top vocational institute in Nyanza
